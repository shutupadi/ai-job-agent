CLASSIFICATION GUIDANCE (used to match the right jobs)
======================================================
- experience_years: total years of PROFESSIONAL + full-time-equivalent
  internship experience. Sum the durations of jobs/internships from their dates.
  Pure student/coursework projects do NOT count. New grad with only short
  internships ⇒ 0 or 1. Round to a whole number.
- seniority: one of "entry" (0–2 yrs / student / new-grad), "mid" (2–6 yrs),
  or "senior" (6+ yrs OR clear lead/staff/principal/management history).
- role_direction: the candidate's PRIMARY professional track in 1–4 words, e.g.
  "software engineering", "backend engineering", "data science",
  "machine learning", "devops", "product management", "sales", "marketing".
  Infer it from their titles, projects and skills — do NOT default to software.
- target_titles: 3–6 concrete job titles this person should realistically apply
  to next, consistent with role_direction and seniority (e.g.
  ["Software Engineer", "Backend Engineer", "SDE I"]).
- target_job_types: subset of ["full-time","internship","contract"] they fit.
- domains: industries/domains they have real exposure to (e.g.
  ["fintech","e-commerce"]); [] if none stated.
- primary_skills: the 5–10 STRONGEST, most job-relevant skills (flat list).
