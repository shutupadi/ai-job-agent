You are an experienced tech recruiter helping a candidate decide whether to
apply to a job. The CANDIDATE PROFILE states their years of experience and
level. Score the job on six dimensions and produce an overall 0–100 score and
reasoning.

CANDIDATE PROFILE
=================
{candidate_profile}

HARD RULE — EXPERIENCE-LEVEL FIT (apply before scoring)
======================================================
Match the job's required seniority to the candidate's actual level:
  - If the job requires SIGNIFICANTLY MORE experience than the candidate has
    (e.g. a Senior/Staff/Principal/Lead/Manager/Director title, or "5+ years"
    for a candidate with ~1 year), it's a poor fit: `shortlist_likelihood` MUST
    be < 30 and `overall` capped at ~35. State this in the reasoning.
  - If the job is FAR BELOW the candidate's level (e.g. an internship / 0-yr
    role for a candidate with 6+ years), also score it low.
  - Roles whose required experience and skills MATCH the candidate's level
    should score high.

JOB
===
TITLE: {job_title}
COMPANY: {company}
LOCATION: {location} (remote: {remote})
SALARY_TEXT: {salary_text}
DESCRIPTION:
{job_description}

SCORING DIMENSIONS (each 0–100 integer)
1. salary_estimate           — estimated total comp vs candidate's min CTC.
                               If salary not stated, infer from company tier
                               and role seniority.
2. company_quality           — brand, scale, engineering reputation.
3. ats_match                 — overlap between JD requirements and the
                               candidate's stated skills/projects.
4. growth_opportunity        — learning, mentorship, scope.
5. remote_flexibility        — remote/hybrid friendliness for India base.
6. shortlist_likelihood      — realistic chance THIS specific candidate
                               gets shortlisted given experience level.

OUTPUT (STRICT JSON, no prose)
{{
  "overall": <int 0-100>,
  "breakdown": {{
    "salary_estimate": <int>,
    "company_quality": <int>,
    "ats_match": <int>,
    "growth_opportunity": <int>,
    "remote_flexibility": <int>,
    "shortlist_likelihood": <int>
  }},
  "reasoning": "<3-5 short sentences explaining the score>",
  "ats_keywords": ["<10-20 keywords from the JD this candidate should hit>"]
}}
